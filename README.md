# Space Shooter

A copy of Space Shooter made in Unity 2d

**How it works:** GamePlay is Simple as the Space Shooter . Player controls his spaceship using mouse and fire bullets toward the enemy spaceship .Player has 3 lifes . And the game gets harder As the user progress. Every 4 wave there willmbe a boss fight.Game gets difficult as the player progress and acheive higher score . Very simple gameplay and Simple mechanism . A simple home screen , pause menu . simple UI system and the score system that display player score . And a PlayerPrefs to store highscore of the player ; 
 
---
 
## Controls

 - - Hold mouse right click for shooting and drag your mouse to the direction you want to go . 

 
## Features
- Boss Fight
- Wave system
- Score System
- Increasing difficulty
- Player highscore 
  
  
Note:- Medium skills required but game gets extremely challanging as player progress.
